About DisasterGuard

DisasterGuard is a trusted educational resource created to promote safety and awareness around natural disasters. We provide clear, practical guidance so individuals and communities can prepare, respond, and recover effectively.

Our Goals

  • Provide accessible, reliable safety information
  • Encourage preparedness and community resilience
  • Share real-life lessons and practical checklists

Why Disaster Matters

Understanding natural disasters and taking proactive steps can save lives. Preparedness empowers communities to recover stronger and reduce the impact of disasters.

Plan

Create a family emergency plan and ensure everyone knows the procedures.

Prepare

Learn
